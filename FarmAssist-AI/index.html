Tomato Early Blight

Tomato
Severity Risk
High (75%)
AI Confidence 94.8%
Pathogen Alternaria solani (Fungus)

Identified Symptoms

Dark, concentric spots resembling targets on older foliage. Leaves yellow around the margins and drop prematurely.

Organic Remedies

  • Apply copper-based organic fungicides every 7–10 days.
  • Prune lower branches of tomato plants to prevent soil-splash pathogens.
  • Mulch around plants to create a barrier between soil spores and leaves.

Chemical Solutions

  • Chlorothalonil or Mancozeb sprays for intensive treatment.
